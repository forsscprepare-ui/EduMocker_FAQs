I forgot my password. How can I change/know it? ▼
Click on the Forgot Password option present on the login/signup page, verify your details and change your password.
I made the payment but my course is not activated yet. What should I do? ▼
After successful payment, if the course is not active, take a screenshot of the payment proof and go to the Ask Doubt section. Send the payment screenshot and request to activate the course. Within 2–4 hours, your course will be activated.

Payment is deducted but showing failed. What should I do? ▼
If payment is deducted but the course is not activated:

Wait for 10–20 minutes.

If payment fails, the amount will automatically be refunded within 3–7 working days.
How can I access my purchased course? ▼
Simply log in using the same registered mobile number & password.

Your purchased course will automatically appear.
